SAFETY COMPLIANCE AUDIT

Site: Parrow Industrial Bottling Plant

Category: Low Heights

FATAL RISK STANDARD LEGISLATION (ZA)

Site Specific Fall Protection Plan

 Corporate Fatal Risk Standards OHSA Constuction Regulations of 2014 (CR) OHSA General Safety Regulations of 1986 (GSR) Occupational Health & Safety Act No. 85 of 1993 (OHSA) Mine Health & Safety Act 29 of 1996 (MHSA)
  • Work on low heights must be controled by using various types of personal fall protection equipment.
  • Working from inside the confines of a Guarded Access Platform.
  • Being connected to the structure via a Low Height FAS.
  • Connected to the structure from the floor/ground.
  • Access the structure whilst connected to the Man-Line and collecting slack on the rope to prevent a fall distance in the event thereof.
  • Collect the maximum slack when on the structure to work inrestraint.
  • Supervision to ensure that low Height FAS remains properly connected and used by the user.
  • Work from a secure, stablished ladder for short periods
